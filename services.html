Check-ups & Hygiene

The foundation of a healthy mouth. A thorough examination, X-rays where needed, and a professional scale & polish to remove plaque and tartar. We'll spot small issues before they become big ones and give you tailored advice to keep your gums healthy at home.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Love your smile again. Professional teeth whitening, natural-looking porcelain veneers and same-day composite bonding to correct chips, gaps and discolouration. We'll talk through what's realistic and design a look that still feels like you.

Whitening from €250*Book now

Fillings & Restorations

Repair and protect. Tooth-coloured, mercury-free fillings that blend in, plus durable crowns, bridges and inlays to rebuild teeth that are worn, cracked or heavily decayed — restoring both comfort and strength.

Fillings from €95*Book now

Orthodontics

Straighter, better-aligned teeth for children, teens and adults. From traditional braces to discreet clear-aligner systems, we'll assess your bite and recommend the most suitable approach — or refer to a specialist orthodontist where appropriate.

Assessment & planBook now

Emergency Dental Care

Toothache, a broken tooth or a lost filling? We keep emergency slots free each day for patients in genuine pain. Call us as early as possible and we'll do our very best to see you the same day and get you comfortable.

Same-day where possibleCall now

Replacing Missing Teeth

Fill the gaps and eat with confidence again. We provide comfortable, well-fitting dentures and fixed bridges, and can arrange dental implant treatment through trusted specialist referral where that's the best option for you.
